## How to Experience Stories

Each story contains:
- **Interactive HTML pages** (page-1.html, page-2.html, etc.)
- **Images** that transform as you explore
- **Story configuration** (story-config.json)

**Getting Started:**
1. Navigate to any story folder
2. Open `page-1.html` in your web browser
3. Click/touch the image to reveal the story
4. Use timeline controls to navigate between pages

## Story Controls

- **Touch/Click**: Reveal parts of the hidden image
- **Influence Slider**: Adjust how much area is affected by touch
- **Trails Toggle**: Enable/disable visual trails while touching
- **Timeline Navigation**: Jump between story pages

## Technical Details

- **Pure HTML/CSS/JavaScript** - No special software required
- **Touch & Mouse Support** - Works on desktop and mobile
- **Canvas-Based Animation** - Smooth image transitions
- **Local Storage** - Your preferences are saved automatically

## Sharing

Stories are best experienced in a modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge).
